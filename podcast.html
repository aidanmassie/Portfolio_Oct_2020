PODCAST




And Other Stuff Podcast

During my first year at NYU, Michael Perrone, a dear friend, and I started a podcast entitled, "And Other Stuff Podcast." This podcast was first conceptualized when Michael was doing a project for a sound imaging class at Tisch. He started filming audio and told me to talk to him. One hour of talking later, we decided we should start a podcast.

And Other Stuff Podcast is a podcast intended for a listener to escape daily troubles and find ease in two college guys talking nonsense for a little under an hour. Some episodes proved to be great, others proved to be less great.

Through this project with Michael, we have been able to discover how to craft the narrative of a podcast episode and its content, how to create something that people want to hear, and how to actually produce a full-scale podcast.
