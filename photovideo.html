VIDEO


I've always had a passion for anything to do with a camera. I tried to make my first film at age 8 and have been constantly trying to improve ever since. I've always wanted to be the guy with the nicest cameras, making the coolest videos that millions of people will watch. I'm not on that level, but I don't think I'm too shabby either.

Below are some of my most recent video works! Give them a look!

Caleb Wallis Highlight Reel

One of my good friends from high school, Caleb Wallis, plays college basketball at the University of Rio Grande. While I was home on a break from school, I got to shoot this video for him at one of Rio Grande's games.



Lisa Leslie Trophy Room Tour

I got to edit this video, shot by the GOAT herself, for the LA Sparks social media accounts during my internship. We used this video as a push for Lisa Leslie votes during the WNBA's GOAT Vote campaign.



New Frontier Outfitters - Only in Appalachia

During my summer internship with Only in Appalachia, I had the privilege of traveling across the northern half of Appalachia, interviewing Appalachians making a difference in their community and sharing their story with the region.

In this video, I interviewed Josh and Jared Ravenscraft, the owners of New Frontier Outfitters, a clothing brand based in Morehead, KY that sells Appalachian-based gear, as well as Kash Daniel, a former University of Kentucky football player.



The Spot on Main Commerical

In my small town in Southern Ohio, my family owns a coffee shop named The Spot on Main. In the summer of 2019, we were looking to attract more teenagers to the shop and create a cool environment for students to connect and do homework. So, we decided to create a thirty second commerical for the shop that aired in the local movie theater before every movie.



NYU Women's Basketball vs. SJFC Highlights

During my brief stint with NYU Atheltics, prior to the COVID-19 pandemic, I created highlight videos of men's and women's basketball games, wrestling matches, and volleyball matches. These videos were edited and completed on the same night as the event, and published the following day on all NYU Athletics social accounts and websites.



Oak Hill Union Football Livestream

One cool thing that came out of me studying remotely during the Fall 2020 semester was the opportunity to livestream a local high school's home football games! I was hired as a freelancer and operated an on-the-field camera, as well as help setup the livestream using professional-level streaming equipment.

The video above is one of the livestreams, where we used three cameras, on-field audio, and audio from the local radio station for play-by-play commentary.
