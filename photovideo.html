VIDEO


I've always had a passion for anything to do with a camera.
I tried to make my first film at age 8 and have been constantly trying
to improve ever since. I've always wanted to be the guy with
the nicest cameras, making the coolest videos that millions of
people will watch. I'm not on that level, but I don't think I'm
too shabby either.

Below are some of my most recent video works! Give them a look!

New Frontier Outfitters - Only in Appalachia

During my summer internship with Only in Appalachia, I had the
privilege of traveling across the northern half of Appalachia,
interviewing Appalachians making a difference in their community
and sharing their story with the region.

In this video, I interviewed Josh and Jared Ravenscraft, the owners
of New Frontier Outfitters, a clothing brand based in Morehead, KY
that sells Appalachian-based gear, as well as Kash Daniel, a former
University of Kentucky football player.



The Spot on Main Commerical

In my small town in Southern Ohio, my family owns a coffee shop
named The Spot on Main. In the summer of 2019, we were looking
to attract more teenagers to the shop and create a cool environment
for students to connect and do homework. So, we decided to create
a thirty second commerical for the shop that aired in the local
movie theater before every movie.



Oak Hill Union Football Livestream

One cool thing that came out of me studying remotely during the
Fall 2020 semester was the opportunity to livestream a local high
school's home football games! I was hired as a freelancer and
operated an on-the-field camera, as well as help setup the livestream
using professional-level streaming equipment.

The video above is one of the livestreams, where we used three
cameras, on-field audio, and audio from the local radio station
for play-by-play commentary.
